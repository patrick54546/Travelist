Description

Located in the south west part of Manila lies an island riddled with beauty and wonder. El Nido has become a world class tourist destination thanks to its white-sand beaches, coral reefs, limestone cliffs and home to a hundred different species of fish.

Tour A - is mostly centered around Miniloc Island, as shown in the map. Tour B - If you want something relaxing where you could enjoy the view, feel the splash and just get your mind off things. Tour B is perfect since you could do the whole tour without swimming and just enjoy the beach.

Tour C - El Nido Island Hopping Tour C is one of the two most popular island hopping tour packages in El Nido Palawan because it really caters to people who just love to swim and explore like the mysterious and abandoned shrine in Matinloc.White sandy beach of Helicopter Island and various other hidden beaches.

Tour D - is the least popular due to Cadlao Islands proximity to the town proper. Some brave backpackers prefers to tour around Cadlao Island by kayak except when the weather is not cooperative for kayaking.
